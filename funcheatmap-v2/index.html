Formula Visualizer

Supported Functions & Operators

  • Basic Operators: +, -, *, /
  • Exponentiation: Use ^ for exponentiation. E.g., a^2 means a squared.
  • Trigonometric Functions: sin(x), cos(x), tan(x)
  • Logarithm: log(x) for natural logarithm
  • Exponential: exp(x) for ex
  • Square Root: sqrt(x)
  • Absolute Value: |x|

You can combine these functions with variables a and b. For example: sqrt(a^2 + b^2) or exp(-a) * cos(b).
