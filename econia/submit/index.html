Commentary template

Your commentary must be written using this template. The template sets out the required fields (title, source, date, word count, syllabus unit, key concept) and the structure your commentary needs to follow. Notice that you need to screenshot the article, not just copy/paste the words, and that you need to highlight the parts you used if the article is much over one page.
