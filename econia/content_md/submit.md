# Submitting it

Stage 10 | The final requirements before the portfolio goes in.

## Commentary template

Your commentary must be written using this template. The template sets out the required fields (title, source, date, word count, syllabus unit, key concept) and the structure your commentary needs to follow. Notice that you need to screenshot the article, not just copy/paste the words, and that you need to highlight the parts you used if the article is much over one page.

[Open the commentary template](https://docs.google.com/document/d/14pCtk3es6D0iCiJzCu2CT4pSc_4pvkCVH5rF6rqc_Ko/edit?usp=sharing)

Copy/paste this template into the document you have been working on.

Submit on the Schoology assignment (in the Internal assessment folder):

- A docx version of your assessment in the format on the template above
- A saved pdf of your article - we may need this later for formal submission.

Remember, this is your first submission. Get it as good as you can; I will be able to give you better feedback if it has already achieved to a high level.
